Crimes In United States





                             
9125971 Total Crimes in 2016

There are two main types of crime in United States, violent crime and property crime. Total crimes is the total of both violent crimes and property crimes.

Violent crimes include aggresive assault, murder manslaughter, rape legacy, rape revised, robbery. Property criems include burglary, larceny, vehicle theft.

In 2016, 86% of crimes were property crimes whereas 14% of crimes were violent crimes. California had the most number of crimes and followed by Texas.


In 2016, larceny is the most commited crime and rape revised is the least commited crime.

For violent crimes, aggresive assault and robbery shows a slight decline from 2001 to 2016 whereas rape legacy and murder is still consistent throughtout the time period.

For property crimes, there is no big change in the rate of burglary and vehicle theft but there were reduced in the number of larceny crime from 2001 to 2016.
